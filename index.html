وَلَقَدْ يَسَّرْنَا الْقُرْآنَ لِلذِّكْرِ فَهَلْ مِن مُّدَّكِرٍ

(The Holy Quran 54:17)

Islamic society of Greater Nashua is honored to host an annual Quran competition on March 28th from 9AM to 6PM for people ages 6-25 years old. The competition is open to the whole community to participate in. Please join us to celebrate our youth in their journey with the Quran.

2025 Quran Competition (Last Year)


Event Schedule

Time Activity
9 am - 9:30 am Registration and Tag Distribution
9:30 am - 10:30 am Levels 1 + 2 Competition
10:30 am - 11:30 am Level 3 Competition
11:30 am - 12:30 pm Level 4 Competition
12:30 pm - 1:30 pm Dhur Prayer + Levels 5, 6, 7 Registration
1:30 pm - 2:30 pm Levels 1, 2, 3, 4 Winner announcements, Awards & Dinner
2:30 pm - 3:30 pm Levels 5 + 6 Competition
3:30 pm - 4:30 pm Level 7 Competition & Asr Prayer
4:30 pm - 5:30 pm Levels 5, 6, 7 Winner Annnouncements and Rewards
7 pm Maghreb Prayer

Important Notes:

  • Levels 1,2,3,4 should be present by 9 am. After 9:30, NO participants of these levels can join.
  • Levels 5,6,7 should be present by 12 pm. After 1:00 pm, NO participants of these levels can join.
  • Schedule is subject to changes based on the number of participans in each level.
  • Dinner will be availabe for $10. Please make sure you have cash.

REGISTRATION

  • Participant Registation

  • Level 1: Surah Al-Fatiha and surahs from Al-Nas to Al-Asr (Up to 8 years of age.)
    Level 2:Surah Al-Takweer & Surah Al-Infitar & Surah Al-Mutaffifeen (age 10 or younger)
    Level 3: Surah Al-Mursalat, Al-Insan (12 or younger)
    Level 4: Al-Jum’ah, Al-Munafiqoon, At-Taghabun (14 or younger)
    Level 5: Al-Waqi’ah, Al-Hadeed (16 or younger)
    Level 6: Muhammed, Al-Fath, Al-Hujurat (18 or younger)
    Level 7: A Full Juz’ of your choice from (Juz’ 1 to 25) (25 or younger)
